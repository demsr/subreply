About this

@subreply was created by @lucian from the desire of a having a simple to use, English-only, public forum that has nothing in common with ancient and untrustworthy social networks.

Features

  1. easy to reply to anyone
  2. save content posted by anyone
  3. ðŸ§ ðŸ’ª one or two emojis instead of avatars
  4. mentions, links and hashtags support
  5. find and follow local or active people
  6. light and dark themes based on OS preference

Limitations

  1. 480 characters per reply
  2. ASCII-only because it works everywhere
  3. unique content, no duplicates, case insensitive
  4. only one mention, link or hashtag per reply
  5. no support for multiple paragraphs
  6. no popularity counters per reply or per profile

Privacy

  1. edit or delete your replies before they are replied to
  2. delete received replies before they are replied to
  3. private following, followers and saved lists
  4. easy to anonymize your personal details
  5. no redistribution of data, private or public (GDPR)
  6. email address needed for password reset, no spam

  1. be nice or else
