Technology

  • Hosted on a high frequency Vultr VPS running lastest Ubuntu Server.
  • Coded in Python using Falcon framework and Django ORM.
  • Traffic handled by using Gunicorn in front of Nginx, PgBouncer in front of PostgreSQL.
